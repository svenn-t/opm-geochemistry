Tutorials

Below are tutorials, and in some cases documentations, for the geochemistry solver itself and the coupling to OPM Flow. The intention is to provide examples of the capabilities of the simulators and provide input files/simulation decks.

Documentation and tutorials for the geochemistry solver

The following tutorials showcase different aspects of the geochemistry solver, with some associated documentation of its internal workings, and is associated with a corresponding keyword(s) for the standalone program GEOCHEMX:

  • Seawater speciation - SOLUTION keyword
  • Equilibrium with exchange sites - IEXCHANGE keyword
  • Equilibrium between seawater, CO2 and calcite - EQUILIBRIUM_PHASES keyword
  • Modeling gas phase at constant volume - GAS_PHASE keyword
  • Surface complexation - COMPLEX keyword
  • Core flooding - RATE and INTERPOLATE keywords
  • 1D reactive transport
  • Advanced reactive transport example: Adsorption of sulphate and cation exchange in core
